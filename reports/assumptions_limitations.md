\# Assumptions and Limitations



\## Assumptions

1\. \*\*Daily Data Sufficiency:\*\* Daily Brent prices adequately capture market trends for strategic decision-making

2\. \*\*Event Timing:\*\* Event dates represent approximate start dates of impact on oil markets

3\. \*\*Market Efficiency:\*\* Oil prices quickly incorporate new information from geopolitical events

4\. \*\*Data Quality:\*\* Provided historical data is accurate and complete

5\. \*\*Model Scope:\*\* Single change point per analysis period is sufficient for initial insights



\## Limitations

1\. \*\*Correlation vs Causation:\*\* Statistical correlation â‰  proven causation

2\. \*\*Omitted Variables:\*\* Many factors (GDP, inflation, weather) not included

3\. \*\*Time Lag Variability:\*\* Event impacts may manifest over different timeframes

4\. \*\*Data Granularity:\*\* Daily data misses intra-day volatility spikes

5\. \*\*Model Simplicity:\*\* Single change point vs multiple regime shifts

6\. \*\*Historical Bias:\*\* Past patterns may not predict future behavior

7\. \*\*Event Selection Bias:\*\* Only 13 major events analyzed
