\# Brent Oil Price Analysis - Final Report



\## Executive Summary

This analysis identified a significant structural break in Brent oil prices on June 2, 2021, with prices increasing by 94.1% following this change point. The Bayesian change point model successfully detected this shift, which correlates with post-pandemic economic recovery.



\## 1. Methodology

\- \*\*Data\*\*: Daily Brent oil prices (1987-2022)

\- \*\*Model\*\*: Bayesian change point detection using PyMC

\- \*\*Events\*\*: 13 key geopolitical/economic events analyzed

\- \*\*Tools\*\*: Python, PyMC, Streamlit dashboard



\## 2. Key Findings

\- \*\*Change Point\*\*: June 2, 2021 (Day 359 in 2020-2022 data)

\- \*\*Price Impact\*\*: Increased from $48.17 to $92.37 average (94.1% rise)

\- \*\*Event Correlation\*\*: Change occurred during post-COVID recovery period

\- \*\*Model Certainty\*\*: High confidence in change point location

\- Daily data misses intra-day volatility
