\# Event Dataset Documentation



\## Structure

\- \*\*Event:\*\* Name of geopolitical/economic event

\- \*\*Date:\*\* Approximate start date of market impact (YYYY-MM-DD)

\- \*\*Type:\*\* Category (Political, Economic, Conflict, Policy, Market)

\- \*\*Description:\*\* Brief impact description (max 200 chars)



\## Event Selection Criteria

1\. \*\*Significance:\*\* Major geopolitical or economic events

2\. \*\*Market Impact:\*\* Documented oil price movement

3\. \*\*Global Relevance:\*\* Affected global oil markets

4\. \*\*Data Availability:\*\* Clear start dates in literature



\## Date Rationale

\- Use approximate start date of market impact

\- For prolonged events (Arab Spring), use initial trigger date

\- For policy announcements, use announcement date

\- For conflicts, use escalation date



\## Sources

\- IMF/World Bank economic reports

\- OPEC announcements

\- Reuters/Bloomberg market analysis

\- Academic literature on oil shocks
